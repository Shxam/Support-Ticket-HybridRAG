Claude Workspace Access and Permissions

Workspace Access Issues:

If you've lost access to a Claude team workspace:
- Only workspace owners and admins can restore access
- Contact your workspace admin or IT department
- Anthropic support cannot override admin decisions
- If you believe this is an error, ask your admin to re-invite you

Workspace Roles:
- Owner: Full control, billing, user management
- Admin: User management, settings (no billing access)
- Member: Standard access, no administrative privileges

If your seat was removed:
- This is an administrative action by your organization
- We cannot restore access without admin approval
- Contact your IT admin or workspace owner
- Provide your email and workspace name

Service Availability:
- If Claude is not responding, check status.anthropic.com
- API failures may be due to rate limits or quota exceeded
- Check your API key validity and permissions
- For AWS Bedrock integration issues, verify IAM permissions and region availability
