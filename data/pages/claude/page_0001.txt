Security, Privacy, and Data Usage

Security Vulnerability Reporting:

If you've found a security vulnerability:
- Report to security@anthropic.com
- Include detailed steps to reproduce
- Do not publicly disclose until we've addressed it
- We have a responsible disclosure policy
- Bug bounty program details at anthropic.com/security

Data Privacy and Usage:

Training Data Opt-in:
- If you've opted in to allow data usage for model improvement
- Data is retained for up to 2 years for training purposes
- You can opt out anytime in Settings → Privacy
- Opting out stops future data collection
- Previously collected data follows our retention policy

Web Crawling:
- To prevent Claude from crawling your website, use robots.txt
- Add: User-agent: ClaudeBot, Disallow: /
- Changes take effect within 24-48 hours
- For immediate removal, contact crawling@anthropic.com
- Provide your domain name and verification of ownership

LTI Integration for Education:

For professors and educational institutions:
- LTI (Learning Tools Interoperability) keys available for Enterprise plans
- Contact education@anthropic.com for setup
- Requires institutional email verification
- Setup takes 3-5 business days
- Student accounts managed through your LMS
