Visa Card Disputes and Chargebacks

Merchant Disputes:

If a merchant sent the wrong product or is unresponsive:
- First, attempt to resolve directly with the merchant
- Visa does not directly process refunds - your card issuer does
- Contact your card-issuing bank to file a dispute
- Provide order details, communication records, and evidence
- Chargeback process takes 45-90 days

How to Dispute a Charge:
1. Contact your card-issuing bank (number on back of card)
2. Explain the issue and provide documentation
3. Bank will investigate and may issue provisional credit
4. Final resolution depends on merchant response
5. Keep all receipts and communication records

Visa's Role:
- Visa provides the payment network
- Your bank (card issuer) handles disputes and refunds
- We cannot ban merchants or process refunds directly
- Merchants are managed by their acquiring banks

Fraud and Unauthorized Charges:
- Report immediately to your card issuer
- Zero liability protection for unauthorized transactions
- Card will be blocked and replaced
- Provisional credit typically issued within 10 business days
