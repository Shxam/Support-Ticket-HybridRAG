Technical Issues and Troubleshooting

Submission Problems:
- If submissions are not working, clear your browser cache
- Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox recommended)
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere
- Check if your code has syntax errors

Apply Tab Not Visible:
- Ensure you're logged in to your account
- Check if you meet the eligibility criteria for the job
- Some positions may have closed applications
- Try refreshing the page or logging out and back in

Compatibility Check Issues:
- Zoom connectivity is required for proctored tests
- Ensure Zoom is installed and updated to the latest version
- Check firewall settings that might block Zoom
- Grant camera and microphone permissions
- System requirements: Windows 10+, macOS 10.13+, stable internet (5 Mbps+)

Test Rescheduling:
- Candidates cannot reschedule tests directly
- Contact the recruiter or company that sent you the test
- HackerRank support cannot reschedule on behalf of companies
- Provide your test link and company name when contacting the recruiter
