Account Access and Permissions

If you've lost access to your HackerRank account, here are the steps to regain access:

1. Password Reset: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
2. Email Verification: Check your email for verification links
3. Account Recovery: Contact support@hackerrank.com with your account details

For workspace access issues:
- Only workspace owners and admins can manage user seats
- If your seat was removed, contact your workspace admin
- We cannot restore access without admin approval

Test and Assessment Access:
- Candidates cannot request score changes or re-evaluation
- Test results are final and determined by the platform's automated grading
- Recruiters make hiring decisions based on test scores
- HackerRank does not intervene in hiring decisions

Mock Interview Issues:
- If mock interviews stop unexpectedly, check your internet connection
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days for technical failures
- Contact support with session ID for refund requests
